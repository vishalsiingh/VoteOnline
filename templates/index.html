Frequently Asked Questions

CampusVote is an innovative online platform designed to facilitate secure, transparent, and efficient campus elections. It empowers students to register as voters, participate in elections, and nominate themselves for leadership positions in the student council. With a focus on security, ease of use, and promoting democratic values, CampusVote is transforming how campus elections are conducted. Whether you're a student, a candidate, or an electoral officer, CampusVote provides a seamless experience, ensuring that every vote counts and every voice is heard.

  • What is CampusVote?

    CampusVote is an online platform designed to simplify campus elections. It allows students to register as voters, nominate themselves for leadership positions, and participate in secure, transparent elections.

  • Who can participate in the elections?

    Only currently enrolled students who have registered as voters on the platform can participate in the elections. Candidates must also meet specific eligibility criteria to run for positions.

  • How do I register as a voter?

    To register as a voter, create an account on the CampusVote platform. After logging in, navigate to the registration section, fill out the necessary details, and submit to complete your registration.

  • Is my vote secure?

    Yes, CampusVote ensures that your vote is private, encrypted, and tamper-proof. We utilize advanced security measures to maintain the integrity and confidentiality of your vote.

  • What if I have technical issues or need help?

    If you experience any problems or need assistance, please visit our Contact Us page or email our support team at support@campusvote.com. We're here to help!
